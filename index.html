Snooker Competition Knock Out or Round Robin Generator

Objective

Create a site that enables users to sign-up for a snooker knock-out or round robin competition

Audience

Front-End:

People who want to sign-up for the competition


Back-End:

Organisers of the competition who can access database of all entrants and generate a knock-out based on the amount of people joining

Requirements

  • Form for entries
  • List of all entrants
  • Generate competition button for admin

Snooker League Table & Grading System

Objective

Create a snooker league system where players can enter results from the matches and submit them for processing. The league table and players' grading will be updated accordingly

Audience

Front-End:

League competitors who will their scores and high breaks.


Back-End:

League organiser who can approve entries and change them accordingly, in case there is an exception

Requirements

  • Table League Display
  • Scores/Results Display
  • High Breaks Display
  • Matches Generator - Matches are set in a distinctive way, 4 players minimum, 7 players maximum each team. Ideally will enable subsitutions but not important for test project
  • Ranking of Players with handicaps, high breaks etc. Must include sorting feature
  • Enter new players(There are always new players who come in as substitutes)
  • (Ideally) Enable a live scoring system

Pot Black Table Accounting System

Objective

Create a table system(similar to the one already implemented) where the table time is calculated and the cost is calculated accordingly. The current one in place has very basic functions, one of them is 'transfer' option, in case someone wants to change tables. The cost is transferred over and continued on th other table

Audience

Front-End:

Employees who can simply switch tables on/off for new customers and calculate the cost.


Back-End:

Centre owner who can access all data for accounting and inventory purposes.

Requirements

  • Graphic Table Display (clickable and keyboard-able)
  • Cost per Time calculator depending on the time spent on the table
  • Specials per person calculator to calculate the cost for the 3-hour special that goes on until 6.30(Cost of table will be compared to cost for the specials -> Cheaper one will be charged)
  • Option to enter amount of people so costs can be split evenly
  • Option to add other costs(food, drink, etc) to the table
  • Option to add people's names to run up a bill that can be paid at the end of their session
  • Ability to add discounts for members or voucher holders
  • Option to transfer tables
